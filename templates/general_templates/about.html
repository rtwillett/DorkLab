Application

The use of advance search operators can be difficult to use, variable across search engines, and challenging for trouble shooting - especially long and complex ones. Our project aims to lower the barrier of entry to using such search methods by detecting the nature of terms added in and streamlining the logic of query construction.

Use cases:
  • Introduction to use of advance search operators
  • Base for cheat cheats and references around such operators
  • Simplicity of construction of operators with numerous terms
